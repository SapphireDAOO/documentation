### Gas and Transaction fees:



* <mark style="color:$primary;">The gas fee</mark> is the fee paid for interaction with the chain. Every interaction, with the exclusion of the payment (made by the buyer), is done by our wallet (the marketplace wallet), each of which requires a gas fee.&#x20;
* The protocol funds this wallet, and its balance shouldn't drop below a certain threshold to ensure transactions can be sent.&#x20;
* These fees are paid in the native token of the Polygon network: POL

***

* <mark style="color:$primary;">Estimated fee per transaction: <$0.01</mark> <mark style="color:$primary;">(except during periods of high network traffic)</mark>

***

<mark style="color:$info;">e.g.</mark>&#x20;

<mark style="color:$info;">10,000 monthly transactions: <$100 per month</mark>

### Data Querying:



* SapphireDAO utilizes The Graph, an indexing protocol for querying data from the blockchain.
* The Graph offers 100k free queries per month, after which the cost is $2 per 100k queries per month, with a slight price reduction for larger purchases.

***

### Chainlink Automation:



* SapphireDAO utilizes Chainlink Automation, a decentralized and reliable way to execute smart contract functions automatically.
